SANTÉ: A Pioneering Approach to Package Design in Health Supplements

My journey into package design began with the SANTÉ project, inspired by the lackluster aesthetics of health supplement containers. Observing their often confusing labels and uninspiring designs, I recognized a unique opportunity to enhance both their legibility and overall visual appeal.

The objective of this project was multifaceted: to create a cohesive logo, innovative package design, and compelling advertisements that not only captured attention but also encouraged potential buyers to engage with the product. By prioritizing clarity and modern aesthetics, I aimed to elevate the consumer experience, making health supplements more accessible and appealing.

Through this endeavor, I sought to redefine the way health products are presented, ultimately bridging the gap between functionality and design.
